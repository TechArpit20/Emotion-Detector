Emotion Recognition in Texts



Emotion Recognition in conversations is an essential frontier due to its ability to mine opinions and detect the underlying emotions from various texts. It helps form different opinions from certain conversations which helps platforms like Twitter, Reddit, YouTube, Facebook etc. It also enables us to provide automated and emotion-aware replies for a dialogue which helps in automated chatbots. We use different factors like number and way of utterances, prior mood, tone , punctuation and several other parameters to detect the emotion.
